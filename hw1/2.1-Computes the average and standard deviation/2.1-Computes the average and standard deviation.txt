					題目說明： Write a function that computes the average and standard deviation of
  input scores. The standard deviation is defined to be the square
  root of the average of the input values: 
(si&nbsp;- m)2&#44; where m is the average of the input scores&nbsp;si&#44; i= 1&#44; …&#44; n. 
				
				
					Note that the number of input
  scores should be dynamic.

					輸出說明：Output the Average and Standard deviation&#44; separated by a tab.
